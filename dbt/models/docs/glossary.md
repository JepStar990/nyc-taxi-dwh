# NYC Taxi Warehouse – Business Glossary

**Trip**: A single pick-up to drop-off journey as recorded by TLC providers.

**Deadhead**: Time/distance between drop-off and next pick-up (computed in Gold shift analysis).

**Airport Trip**: A trip whose PU or DO zone is JFK (132), LGA (138), or EWR (1), or rate codes 2/3.

**CBD Congestion Fee**: Per-trip charge for MTA’s Congestion Relief Zone (effective 2025‑01‑05).

**Data Quality Score**: Composite score (0–100) summarizing temporal, spatial, and financial validity checks.

**Suspicious Trip**: A record flagged for potential fraud/anomaly (e.g., zero-distance fare, extreme speed).
